Our Story

This cap is designed by a passionate female entrepreneur with a mission to give back. Growing up, I faced a difficult childhood and promised myself I’d help children who lack support, so 5% of every sale is donated to kids in need. My journey also inspired me to support pets, after seeing many unable to afford care for their furry friends while my own pet was in the hospital. Another 5% of proceeds goes toward helping pets get the treatment they deserve. By purchasing this cap, you’re not just buying a product—you’re supporting a cause to create a brighter future for kids and pets alike.
